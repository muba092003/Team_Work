What is color therapy?

Color therapy is a gentle wellness practice that uses the symbolic and emotional power of color to encourage healing, balance, and self-awareness. Each hue can bring a different feeling, from calm and clarity to creativity and joy.

It can be practiced through light, décor, clothing, meditation, and mindful visualization.

How it helps

  • Supports relaxation and stress relief
  • Encourages emotional balance
  • Boosts creativity and confidence
  • Creates a soothing atmosphere at home or work
